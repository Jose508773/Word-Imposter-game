🕵️ Word Imposter

One player is secretly the imposter. Can you figure out who?

🕵️ How to Play:

  • Everyone gets a card with the same word (except the imposter)
  • The imposter gets a different word and must lie to blend in
  • Take turns giving clues about your word
  • 🔍 Watch for suspicious behavior and inconsistent clues
  • 🤥 The imposter must deceive without being caught
  • ⚖️ Vote to identify the imposter
